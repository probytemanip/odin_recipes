Salted Caramel Apple shots

Description

Salted caramel apple shots capture the flavors of fall in a glass. They are delightfully sweet and boozy. Taking a little bite of apple at the end adds the nostalgic flavor of a carnival caramel apple.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons caramel sauce, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon flaky sea salt (such as Maldon Sea SaltÂ®)
  • ice as needed
  • 3 ounces vodka
  • 4 ounces apple cider
  • 1 teaspoon fresh squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
  • green apple slices, for garnish

Steps

  1. Reserve 2 teaspoons caramel sauce. Dip one side of the rim of each shot glass in remaining caramel and sprinkle each with about 1/8 teaspoon salt.
  2. Fill cocktail shaker half way with ice. Add vodka, apple cider, lemon juice, cinnamon, and remaining caramel sauce. Shake well and pour into prepared shot glasses. Garnish with a small apple slice if desired.
